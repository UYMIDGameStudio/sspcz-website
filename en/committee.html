Committee & Support

The conference is organized by students, with joint support from many secondary school societies across Zhejiang in every session.

01 — THE 3RD SESSION

Structure of the 3rd Session

HOST
Organizing Committee of the 3rd SSPCZ · the Weijing Initiative (未竟计划)
CO-ORGANIZER
Hangzhou Global Youth Talent Center (more to be announced)
ACADEMIC SUPPORT
"Digital & Humanities" Research Center, Nanjing Normal University
STRUCTURE
The Secretariat (Secretary-General and Deputy Secretary-General) is the committee's decision-making body, responsible for overall coordination and direction. Under it are the Publicity, Academic, Conference Affairs, Outreach and Finance teams, each led by a coordinator, with 10–15 members working together.
SUPPORTING SOCIETIES
Hangzhou Foreign Languages School: Philosophy · Psychology · History · Interdisciplinary · Gufeng · English Debate · Art History · Law · Xixi Literature Societies
Hangzhou Literature Union · Zhengze Chinese Classics Society (Hangzhou No.14 High School) · Taoli Literature Society (Hangzhou No.2 High School) · Binghuo Literature Society (Yanzhou High School) · Fanxing Literature Society (Xuejun High School) · Shanyou Langqing Poetry Society · Aojiang High School Literature Society (Wenzhou) · Chunqiu Literature Society (Pinghu High School, Zhejiang)
