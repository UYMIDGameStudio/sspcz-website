01 — ABOUT

About the Conference

The Secondary School Philosophy Conference of Zhejiang (SSPCZ) is an open academic forum for middle and high school students passionate about philosophy. We aim to create a festival of ideas and exchange — inviting experts as guest speakers and combining lectures with student paper presentations to bring philosophy to every participant and kindle a spirit of inquiry among secondary school students.

The first two sessions were successfully held in a hybrid offline-and-online format, welcoming Prof. Jiang Yuhui, Wei Ran, Wu Jing, Hu Xingming, Lin Wei and other experts from a range of fields for keynote lectures. With strong support from high-school philosophy societies across Hangzhou and enthusiastic student participation, the conference continues to advance philosophy among secondary school students.
