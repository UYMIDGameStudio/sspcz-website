# RFC-000: Vision and Narrative

## 1. Identity (Who are we?)
The Secondary School Philosophy Conference of Zhejiang (SSPCZ) is not merely an annual event, but an ongoing dialectic community. Correspondingly, this repository does not build an "event website," but a **Digital Publication Platform**. 

## 2. Philosophical Stance (What thinking do we encourage?)
We emphasize philosophy as a practice rather than a relic. We encourage thinking that is:
- **Unfolding (Ateleios)**: Ideas here are in a state of continuous formation, not finalized dogma. 
- **Dialogical**: Rooted in questioning, responding, and mutual critique.
- **Rigorous**: Adhering to strict academic standards and logic, even at the secondary school level.

## 3. Platform Expression (How does the platform express this?)
The platform must physically embody this philosophical stance. Every digital issue (edition of the conference) acts as a scholarly journal publication. The interface should feel like an active space of unfolding thought—a desk with an open, annotated book—rather than a marketing landing page or a museum of finished knowledge.
