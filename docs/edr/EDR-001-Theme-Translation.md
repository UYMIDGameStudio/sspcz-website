# EDR-001: Canonical English Rendering of the Issue III Theme

- **Status**: Accepted (secretariat ruling, 2026-07-08)
- **Type**: Editorial Decision Record
- **Scope**: All English-language content, site-wide, from Phase 2 onward

## Decision

The theme 「变与不变」 is rendered in English as:

> **Change & Invariance**

## Rejected alternatives

| Candidate | Origin | Reason for rejection |
|-----------|--------|----------------------|
| "Change & the Unchanging" | Legacy static site | Literary translation; nominalizes the negative ("the Unchanging") rather than naming the concept |
| "Change and Permanence" | RFC-200 §2 illustrative example | Standard literary pairing; does not carry the intended theoretical weight |

## Rationale

"Invariance" reflects the conference's theoretical ontology — the dynamic
dialectics of knowledge-system evolution — rather than a standard literary
translation. The term names what persists *as a property under
transformation* (as in invariants preserved across change), which is the
precise philosophical claim the theme letter develops: grasping the
constant law within change, and making room for the force of change within
the constant.

## Application rules

1. Every English occurrence of the theme uses exactly **"Change &
   Invariance"** — including editorial documents, structured data values,
   UI micro-copy, and metadata.
2. The Chinese theme remains 「变与不变」 and is authoritative (SSOT).
3. RFC-200 §2's "Change and Permanence" is henceforth read as an
   illustrative example of capitalization style only, superseded by this
   record for the actual theme name.
